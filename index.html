Cindy Ho

About Me

My name is Cindy Ho and I am a 21-year-old technology enthusiast. I am currently studying Multimedia Technology - Virtual Production at Karel de Grote University College in Belgium.
I truly believe that Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality can help people in many ways.
What I'm most excited about are the endless possibilities these apps can bring
in the health and education sector.
Helping people learn and improve themselves through technology is something I am very passionate about.
I would love to be able to contribute to positive social change worldwide.

What I Do
Unity Developer

I have made several small projects in Unity, both in 2D and 3D. I would like to specialize in making Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality apps because I believe that these apps will have a big influence in almost every sector in the future.

3D Artist

3D is used in a lot of games and apps to make it more realistic and immersive for the user. This is why it is important to be able to create or at least have some knowledge about 3D models. The 3D software that I use is Blender.

Digital Marketing

I have helped a NGO called Rikolto with their digital marketing in the Google Ad Grants programme. The platforms that I have used are Google Adwords and Google Analytics.



UI/UX Design

The user has to have an enjoyable experience while using your application. For this reason I have some experience with UI/UX design in websites and apps. Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator are the softwares that I use.

Photography

Taking pictures is something I really enjoy in my spare time. I am still an amateur photographer, but I'll take every chance to capture beautiful sceneries around the world and special moments with my friends and family.

Piano

During my free time I also like to play the piano. It helps me to relax, to ignore the worries and just enjoy the moment.
